About Me

See Xiong is a web developer student, challenging herself to innovate the future with software development. See had always had a passion for arts, fine designs, and problem solving. In 12th grade, she took a computer coding class to fulfill for an elective course and was introduced to her first programing language - Javascript. After high school, See worked as a program coordinator with multicultural communities and performing arts. In May 2017, she obtained her associate degree in Medical Office and became an office assistant at a home-health care facility. In Fall 2018, See pursed her childhood dream and received full-stack, formal training from Trilogy Coding Bootcamp.
