About Me

See Xiong is a full stack web developer with a background in performing arts and medical office. She is skilled in conceptualizing, designing, and developing logical solutions to real-life problems.

See had always had a passion for fine designs, computer coding, and problem solving. In 12th grade, she took a computer coding class to fulfill for an elective course and was introduced to her first programing language - JavaScript.

After high school, See worked as a program coordinator with multicultural communities and performing arts. In May 2017, she obtained her associate degree in Medical Office and provided office assistance at a home health care business. In Fall 2018, See pursed her childhood dream and received formal training for full stack web developement from Trilogy University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp. She has a saying of “Be the leader of yourself” and is committed to lifelong learning.
