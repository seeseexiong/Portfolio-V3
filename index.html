About Me

See Xiong is a full stack web developer with a background in performing arts and medical office. She is skilled in conceptualizing, designing, and developing logical solutions to real-life problems.

See has always had a passion for computer coding, fine designs, and problem solving. In 12th grade, she took a computer coding class to fulfill for an elective course and was introduced to her first programing language - JavaScript.

After high school, See worked as a program coordinator with multicultural communities and performing arts. In May 2017, she obtained her associate degree in Medical Office and provided office assistance at a home health care business.

In fall 2018, See pursed her childhood dream and received formal training for full stack web developement from Trilogy University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp. See is committed to lifelong learning with a motto to be the leader of herself.
